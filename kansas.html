About Safety Research in Kansas

Kansas's safety statistics are concentrated around Wichita, the state's largest city, and the Kansas City metro area that straddles the Kansas-Missouri border. The Overland Park area in particular has emerged as one of the more affluent and rapidly growing suburban communities in the broader Kansas City metro.

Wichita's economy, historically tied to aviation manufacturing, has shaped a safety profile typical of many mid-sized Midwestern industrial cities. Rural Kansas, which makes up the vast majority of the state's geography, shows markedly different crime patterns than its urban centers.
