About Safety Research in Alabama

Alabama's major metros — Birmingham, Montgomery, and Huntsville — each reflect different economic bases that shape their safety landscapes. Huntsville's strong aerospace and technology sector has supported significant growth and generally favorable safety trends in its surrounding suburbs, while Birmingham's longer industrial history has created more variation between its urban core and surrounding communities.

As the state capital, Montgomery's safety profile reflects a mix of government employment and historic neighborhoods, with patterns typical of many mid-sized Southern state capitals. Suburban growth around all three major Alabama metros continues to reshape local safety statistics.
