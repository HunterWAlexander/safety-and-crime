About Safety Research in North Carolina

North Carolina has experienced some of the fastest population growth of any state over the past decade, concentrated heavily in the Charlotte and Raleigh-Durham metro areas. This growth has been driven by a strong banking and technology sector presence, particularly around the Research Triangle, which has reshaped safety patterns as new suburban developments continue to emerge.

Charlotte's rapid growth has created notable variation between its established neighborhoods like Myers Park and newer developing areas, while Raleigh and Durham's tech-driven economy has supported generally favorable safety trends in much of the metro area compared to historic urban cores.
