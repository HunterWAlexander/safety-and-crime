About Safety Research in Minnesota

Minnesota's safety statistics are concentrated heavily in the Minneapolis-Saint Paul metro area, often called the Twin Cities. Minneapolis and Saint Paul each have distinct neighborhood characters, with safety patterns shaped by the region's strong economy and well-regarded public services relative to many other Midwestern metros.

Rochester, home to the Mayo Clinic, represents a notably different economic profile centered around healthcare and medical tourism. Minnesota generally shows favorable statewide safety trends, partly reflecting strong social services infrastructure throughout the state.
