About Safety in San Jose

San Jose sits at the heart of Silicon Valley and is the tenth-largest city in the U.S. As one of the wealthiest major metros in the country, San Jose generally shows stronger safety profiles than many comparably sized cities, particularly in residential neighborhoods like Almaden Valley and Willow Glen.

San Jose's high cost of living and tech-driven economy have shaped a city with relatively low density sprawl compared to other major California cities, which tends to correlate with more favorable safety scores across much of the metro area.
