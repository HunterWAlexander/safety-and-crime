About Safety Research in Iowa

Iowa's safety landscape is shaped by its two largest metros, Des Moines and Cedar Rapids, both of which have benefited from diversified economies tied to insurance, agriculture, and technology sectors. Des Moines in particular has seen significant growth and downtown revitalization, generally correlating with favorable safety trends.

Iowa's predominantly rural character outside its major metros means safety patterns across much of the state reflect smaller, close-knit communities rather than typical urban crime statistics. This rural character contributes to Iowa generally showing favorable statewide safety trends compared to more urbanized states.
