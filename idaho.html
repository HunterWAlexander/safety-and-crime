About Safety Research in Idaho

Idaho has experienced significant population growth in recent years, particularly around Boise, which has emerged as one of the fastest-growing metro areas in the country. This growth has generally correlated with favorable safety trends in newer suburban developments throughout the Treasure Valley region.

Idaho's relatively low overall population density compared to most states means many ZIP codes outside the Boise metro show very different safety characteristics, often reflecting small-town or rural community dynamics rather than urban crime patterns.
