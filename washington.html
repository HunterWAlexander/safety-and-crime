About Safety Research in Washington

Washington's safety statistics are dominated by the Seattle metro area, which has seen significant growth and gentrification tied to the technology industry over the past two decades. This growth has reshaped many central Seattle neighborhoods, while Spokane, on the state's eastern side, shows a notably different economic profile and safety pattern.

Tacoma, part of the broader Puget Sound region, represents yet another distinct community within Washington's safety landscape. The state's geographic divide between the wetter, more urbanized west and drier eastern regions contributes to meaningfully different safety trends across Washington.
