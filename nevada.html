About Safety Research in Nevada

Nevada's safety statistics are dominated by the Las Vegas metro area, which has a unique economy built around tourism, entertainment, and hospitality. The Strip and surrounding tourist areas see distinct crime patterns related to high visitor traffic, while residential suburbs like Henderson show notably different and often more favorable safety profiles.

Reno, the state's second-largest metro, has seen significant economic diversification beyond gaming in recent years, generally correlating with evolving safety trends. Nevada's rapid population growth continues to reshape ZIP codes throughout both metro areas.
