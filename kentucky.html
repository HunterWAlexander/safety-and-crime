About Safety Research in Kentucky

Kentucky's safety landscape is anchored by Louisville and Lexington, two metros with distinct economic identities tied respectively to logistics/manufacturing and horse racing/bourbon industries. Louisville's larger size and more diverse economy has created more safety variation across its neighborhoods compared to the more compact Lexington metro.

Kentucky's bourbon and horse racing industries have shaped unique tourism dynamics in and around Lexington, while Louisville's role as a logistics hub has supported different growth patterns. Both cities show typical variation between urban cores and surrounding suburban communities.
