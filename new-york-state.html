About Safety Research in New York State

New York State is anchored by New York City but also includes diverse upstate regions like Buffalo, Albany, and the Hudson Valley, each with very different safety profiles. NYC's five boroughs see significant variation between dense Manhattan ZIP codes and more residential areas in the outer boroughs, while upstate cities like Buffalo and Albany tend to show patterns more typical of mid-sized Rust Belt cities.

New York's population density is among the highest of any state, concentrated heavily in the NYC metro area, which means ZIP-code-level research is especially valuable given how much safety can vary within just a few blocks in the city. Upstate New York generally sees lower population density and correspondingly different crime patterns compared to the five boroughs.
