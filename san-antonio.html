About Safety in San Antonio

San Antonio is the seventh-largest city in the U.S. and one of the fastest-growing major metros in Texas. The city's growth has been concentrated in suburban areas like Stone Oak and the area around UTSA, which tend to show stronger safety profiles compared to the historic downtown core near the Alamo.

Like many Texas cities, San Antonio's sprawling geography means safety experiences can vary significantly between the established West Side neighborhoods and newer suburban developments on the city's growing edges.
