About Safety Research in New Hampshire

New Hampshire's lack of state income or sales tax has historically attracted residents from neighboring Massachusetts, particularly in southern communities like Manchester and Nashua near the Massachusetts border. This proximity to the Boston metro has shaped distinct economic and safety dynamics compared to more rural northern parts of the state.

New Hampshire generally shows favorable statewide safety statistics, partly reflecting its smaller overall population and strong sense of close-knit New England communities throughout much of the state.
