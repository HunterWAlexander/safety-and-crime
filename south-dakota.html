About Safety Research in South Dakota

South Dakota's safety landscape is anchored by Sioux Falls, by far the state's largest city, and Rapid City near the Black Hills region. Sioux Falls has seen significant growth tied to a diversified financial services and healthcare economy, generally correlating with favorable safety trends.

South Dakota's predominantly rural character outside these two metros contributes to generally favorable statewide safety statistics typical of many Great Plains states with lower overall population density.
