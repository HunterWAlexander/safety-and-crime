About Safety Research in Tennessee

Tennessee's safety statistics are split between Nashville, Memphis, and Knoxville, three metros with distinctly different economic identities tied respectively to music/healthcare, logistics/music, and higher education/manufacturing. Nashville's rapid growth over the past decade has reshaped safety patterns considerably in many of its central neighborhoods.

Memphis has a different demographic and economic history than Nashville, with safety patterns reflecting its role as a major logistics hub. Knoxville, anchored by the University of Tennessee, shows yet another distinct profile typical of college-driven Southern cities.
