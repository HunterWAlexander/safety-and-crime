About Safety Research in Oklahoma

Oklahoma's safety landscape is anchored by Oklahoma City and Tulsa, its two largest metros, both of which have economies historically tied to energy production alongside growing diversification in recent decades. Oklahoma City's downtown revitalization (Bricktown and surrounding areas) has generally correlated with improving safety trends in its urban core.

Tulsa's distinct arts and cultural districts, including the historic Greenwood neighborhood, reflect a different economic and social history than Oklahoma City. Both metros show typical variation between urban cores and favorable suburban safety statistics.
