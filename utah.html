About Safety Research in Utah

Utah's safety landscape is concentrated heavily along the Wasatch Front corridor connecting Salt Lake City, Provo, and surrounding suburbs. The state has experienced significant population growth in recent years, generally correlating with favorable safety trends in many newer suburban developments throughout the corridor.

St. George, in the state's southern region, represents a distinct retirement and tourism-driven economy compared to the Salt Lake City metro. Utah generally shows favorable statewide safety statistics relative to many other Western states.
