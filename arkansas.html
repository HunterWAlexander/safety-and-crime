About Safety Research in Arkansas

Arkansas's safety statistics are concentrated heavily around Little Rock, the state capital and largest metro area, along with the rapidly growing Northwest Arkansas region around Fayetteville and Bentonville. Northwest Arkansas has seen significant economic growth tied to major corporate headquarters, generally correlating with favorable safety trends in newer suburban developments.

Little Rock's safety profile reflects a mix of government and historic neighborhoods typical of many Southern state capitals, with meaningful variation between its urban core and surrounding suburban communities.
