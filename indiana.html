About Safety Research in Indiana

Indiana's safety statistics are anchored by Indianapolis, the state's capital and largest city, along with secondary metros like Fort Wayne and Evansville. Indianapolis has seen significant downtown revitalization in recent decades, though safety patterns still vary considerably between its urban core and surrounding suburban communities.

Indiana's strong manufacturing heritage has shaped many of its mid-sized cities, with safety trends often tied to broader regional economic patterns common throughout the industrial Midwest. Suburban growth around Indianapolis continues to create new ZIP codes with generally favorable safety profiles.
