About Safety Research in Wisconsin

Wisconsin's safety statistics are anchored by Milwaukee, the state's largest city, along with Madison, the state capital and home to the University of Wisconsin. Milwaukee's manufacturing heritage has shaped a safety profile with more neighborhood-level variation compared to the more compact, university-driven Madison metro.

Green Bay, known nationally for its NFL franchise, represents a smaller and more close-knit community compared to Milwaukee, generally showing favorable safety statistics typical of many mid-sized Wisconsin cities.
