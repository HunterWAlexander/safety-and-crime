About Safety Research in Connecticut

Connecticut's safety landscape is shaped by its proximity to both New York City and Boston, with significant variation between its urban centers like Hartford and New Haven and its affluent suburban communities closer to the coast. Hartford's role as the state capital and insurance industry hub creates a safety profile typical of many smaller Northeastern cities.

Connecticut's high population density relative to its small geographic size means safety can vary noticeably even within short distances, particularly between urban centers and the wealthy suburban communities that surround them throughout Fairfield County and beyond.
