About Safety in Philadelphia

Philadelphia is one of the oldest major cities in the United States, with a dense urban core surrounded by historic residential neighborhoods. Center City and the immediate downtown area see typical urban crime patterns, while neighborhoods like Chestnut Hill and parts of Northwest Philadelphia tend to show stronger safety profiles due to lower density and different housing stock.

Philadelphia's tight grid of historic neighborhoods means safety can change noticeably within just a few blocks, making ZIP-code-level research particularly valuable for anyone evaluating a move to the city or its immediate suburbs.
