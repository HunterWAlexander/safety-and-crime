About Safety Research in Virginia

Virginia's safety landscape spans from the D.C.-adjacent suburbs of Arlington to the coastal communities of Virginia Beach and the state capital of Richmond. Northern Virginia's proximity to Washington D.C. has created some of the most affluent and consistently safe suburban communities in the entire state, driven by federal employment.

Richmond's distinct history and ongoing revitalization efforts have reshaped safety patterns in its urban core in recent years, while Virginia Beach's coastal tourism economy creates yet another distinct safety profile compared to the rest of the state.
