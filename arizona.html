About Safety Research in Arizona

Arizona has been one of the fastest-growing states in the country, with the Phoenix metro area absorbing much of that growth through extensive suburban development in cities like Scottsdale, Gilbert, and Chandler. This rapid expansion has generally correlated with favorable safety trends in newer planned communities, while older parts of central Phoenix show more typical urban crime patterns.

Tucson, the state's second-largest metro, has a somewhat different growth trajectory than Phoenix, with safety patterns shaped by its university presence and distinct desert geography. Arizona's continued population growth means new ZIP codes continue to emerge across both metro areas.
