About Safety & Crime

Instant safety insights for any U.S. ZIP code — free, fast, and easy to understand.

What We Do

SafetyAndCrime.com is a free tool that lets anyone instantly look up safety scores, crime statistics, home values, population data, and more for any U.S. ZIP code. Whether you're thinking about moving to a new neighborhood, researching a city, or just curious about your area — we make it easy to get a quick safety overview in seconds.

What You Get With Every Search

  • Safety Score — A 0–100 score showing how safe a ZIP code is relative to others across the U.S.
  • Violent Crime Rate — Estimated violent crimes per 100,000 residents, with a breakdown by type
  • Property Crime Rate — Estimated property crimes per 100,000 residents, with a breakdown by type
  • Crime Trend — Whether crime is rising, stable, or declining in the area
  • Population — Estimated population and neighborhood type (Urban, Suburb, Rural, etc.)
  • Median Home Value — Estimated home values and market classification
  • Risk Level — Low, Medium, or High risk classification
  • Interactive Map — Visual map showing the exact location with a color-coded risk marker

Our Data

Regional Crime Index — Our safety scores and crime statistics are based on regional historical averages derived from FBI Uniform Crime Reporting (UCR) data. Scores are calculated using a regional index model that accounts for geographic crime patterns across the United States.
Location Data — City names, state information, and map coordinates are provided by Zippopotam.us, a free ZIP code lookup service.
Home Value & Population Estimates — Based on regional Census Bureau and historical real estate data averages by ZIP prefix region.
